1. Purpose

This policy outlines the ethical principles that guide our digital actions and services to create a safe, inclusive, and responsible learning environment.

2. Core Principles

  • Respect: Treat all people with fairness, dignity, and respect online and offline.
  • Privacy: Protect user data and uphold digital confidentiality.
  • Transparency: Provide clear, truthful digital content and terms.
  • Inclusion: Enable digital access for women and marginalized groups.
  • Well-being: Promote healthy, balanced tech use.
  • Intellectual Property: Respect copyrights and attribute creators.
  • Responsible Tech: Ensure AI and digital tools are used ethically.

3. Expectations

All learners and staff must act responsibly, refrain from digital harm, and report abuse or violations.

4. Reporting

Email ai@mezimmah.com to report any policy violations. Confidentiality is assured.

5. Policy Review

Reviewed annually or when major digital or legal changes occur.
